About Us

Prince DrK Vasudevan college of Engineering and Technology, established in 2009, is a premier institution dedicated to excellence in engineering education and research. Located in a serene environment, the college offers state-of-the-art facilities and a vibrant campus life. Our mission is to nurture innovative minds and equip students with the skills needed to excel in the ever-evolving field of technology. With a team of experienced faculty members and industry collaborations, we strive to provide a holistic learning experience that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and professional growth.
